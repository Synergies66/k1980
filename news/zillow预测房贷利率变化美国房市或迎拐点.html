Zillow预测房贷利率变化，美国房市或迎拐点

Zillow Predicts Mortgage Rate Changes, US Housing Market May Reach Inflection Point

K1980 深度 · 2026年03月28日
房地产巨头Zillow发布最新预测，认为美国房贷利率将在2024年下半年开始回落，房价涨幅放缓但不会大跌。这一预测对购房者、投资者和相关从业者都将产生重要影响，特别是那些等待入市时机的首次购房者和房地产投资人。
Real estate giant Zillow released its latest forecast, predicting that US mortgage rates will begin to decline in the second half of 2024, with home price appreciation slowing but avoiding major declines. This prediction will have significant implications for homebuyers, investors, and industry professionals, particularly first-time homebuyers and real estate investors waiting for the right market timing.

Zillow核心预测解读

房地产数据巨头Zillow近期发布了对美国房贷利率和住房市场的最新预测，认为经历了2022-2023年的大幅波动后，市场正逐步趋于稳定。该公司预计房贷利率将从目前的7%以上水平逐步回落至6.5%左右，但仍远高于疫情期间的历史低位。

房价方面，Zillow预测全国房价涨幅将从2023年的高位回落，但仍将保持正增长，预计年涨幅在2-4%之间。这意味着房价不会出现大幅下跌，但增速将明显放缓。

对购房者的实际影响

对于普通购房者而言，这一预测释放了积极信号。首先，房贷利率的预期下降将直接降低购房成本。以50万美元房贷为例，利率从7.5%降至6.5%，30年期月供可减少约280美元，总利息支出减少超过10万美元。

然而，购房者仍需面对挑战。房价的持续上涨意味着购房门槛依然较高，特别是在西海岸和东北部热门城市。建议购房者根据自身财务状况合理规划，避免过度杠杆。

投资者策略调整

对房地产投资者来说，Zillow的预测提供了重要的市场参考。利率下降将改善房地产投资的现金流状况，特别是对于依赖融资的投资者。同时，房价涨幅放缓意味着投资回报率可能不如前几年火热。

机构投资者可能会重新评估投资组合，从追求快速增值转向注重租金收益的稳健策略。个人投资者则需要更加谨慎地选择地段和物业类型，优质学区房和交通便利区域仍将是热点。

相关行业从业者影响

房地产经纪人、贷款经纪人等从业者将面临市场结构性变化。利率下降可能刺激更多潜在买家入市，增加交易量，但单笔交易的佣金收入增长可能放缓。

建筑和装修行业也将受到影响。新房建设可能因融资成本下降而增加，但房价涨幅放缓可能抑制高端装修需求。从业者需要调整业务重点，更多关注性价比和实用性。

移民群体特殊考量

对于在美工作的移民群体，房贷利率变化具有特殊意义。H1B、EB类移民等群体往往面临身份不确定性，高利率环境下更难获得优惠贷款条件。利率预期下降为这一群体提供了更好的购房窗口期。

但移民购房者仍需注意，银行对非永久居民的贷款要求通常更严格，需要更高的首付比例和更稳定的收入证明。建议提前准备相关文件，选择对移民友好的贷款机构。

风险提示与前瞻

尽管Zillow的预测相对乐观，但市场仍存在不确定性。通胀数据、就业市场变化、地缘政治因素都可能影响美联储货币政策，进而影响房贷利率走势。

此外，不同地区的房地产市场分化将更加明显。科技中心、金融中心等高收入地区可能继续保持相对强势，而部分中小城市可能面临更大调整压力。投资者和购房者都需要密切关注局部市场动态，做出符合自身情况的理性决策。

Interpreting Zillow's Core Predictions

Real estate data giant Zillow recently released its latest forecast on US mortgage rates and the housing market, believing that after significant fluctuations in 2022-2023, the market is gradually stabilizing. The company predicts mortgage rates will gradually decline from current levels above 7% to around 6.5%, but will remain far above the historic lows seen during the pandemic.

Regarding home prices, Zillow predicts that national home price appreciation will decline from 2023 highs but will maintain positive growth, with annual appreciation expected between 2-4%. This means home prices won't experience sharp declines, but growth will noticeably slow.

Practical Impact on Homebuyers

For ordinary homebuyers, this forecast sends positive signals. First, the expected decline in mortgage rates will directly reduce the cost of homeownership. For example, with a $500,000 mortgage, a rate drop from 7.5% to 6.5% would reduce monthly payments by approximately $280, saving over $100,000 in total interest over 30 years.

However, homebuyers still face challenges. Continued home price appreciation means the threshold for homeownership remains high, especially in hot cities on the West Coast and Northeast. Homebuyers are advised to plan rationally based on their financial situation and avoid excessive leverage.

Investment Strategy Adjustments

For real estate investors, Zillow's forecast provides important market reference points. Interest rate declines will improve cash flow conditions for real estate investments, particularly for investors relying on financing. Simultaneously, slowing home price appreciation means investment returns may not be as robust as in previous years.

Institutional investors may re-evaluate their portfolios, shifting from pursuing rapid appreciation to focusing on stable rental income strategies. Individual investors need to be more cautious in selecting locations and property types, with homes in quality school districts and conveniently located areas likely remaining popular.

Impact on Related Industry Professionals

Real estate agents, mortgage brokers, and other professionals will face structural market changes. Declining rates may stimulate more potential buyers to enter the market, increasing transaction volume, but commission income growth per transaction may slow.

The construction and renovation industries will also be affected. New home construction may increase due to lower financing costs, but slowing home price appreciation may suppress high-end renovation demand. Industry professionals need to adjust business focus, paying more attention to cost-effectiveness and practicality.

Special Considerations for Immigrant Groups

For immigrant workers in the US, mortgage rate changes hold special significance. Groups such as H1B and EB-class immigrants often face immigration status uncertainty, making it harder to obtain favorable loan terms in high-rate environments. Expected rate declines provide a better window for this group to purchase homes.

However, immigrant homebuyers should note that banks typically have stricter lending requirements for non-permanent residents, requiring higher down payments and more stable income documentation. It's recommended to prepare relevant documents in advance and select lenders that are immigrant-friendly.

Risk Warnings and Outlook

Despite Zillow's relatively optimistic forecast, market uncertainty remains. Inflation data, employment market changes, and geopolitical factors may all influence Federal Reserve monetary policy, which in turn affects mortgage rate trends.

Additionally, real estate market differentiation across regions will become more pronounced. High-income areas such as technology centers and financial hubs may maintain relative strength, while some small and medium-sized cities may face greater adjustment pressures. Both investors and homebuyers need to closely monitor local market dynamics and make rational decisions suited to their individual circumstances.
