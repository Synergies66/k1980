US Mortgage Rates Rise Again: Where Do Homebuyers Go From Here

K1980 深度 · 2026年03月28日
美国房贷利率持续上升，30年期固定房贷利率已攀升至7%以上，创20年新高。这一变化正深刻影响房地产市场，购房成本大幅增加，市场活跃度显著下降。对于有购房需求的读者，需要重新评估购房时机和策略。
US mortgage rates continue to surge, with 30-year fixed-rate mortgages climbing above 7%, reaching a 20-year high. This shift is profoundly impacting the real estate market, significantly increasing homebuying costs and notably reducing market activity. For readers with homebuying needs, it is necessary to reassess timing and strategy for purchasing a home.

房贷利率飙升至历史高位

美国房贷利率正经历近20年来最为剧烈的上涨。据房地美数据显示，30年期固定房贷利率已突破7%关口，相比2021年的历史低点2.65%，涨幅超过160%。这一急剧变化源于美联储持续加息政策以及通胀压力的持续存在。

对于普通购房者而言，这意味着购房成本的大幅增加。以50万美元房屋为例，按20%首付计算，当前7%的利率将使月供达到约2,661美元，而在2021年2.65%利率下，同样条件的月供仅为1,616美元，差距超过1,000美元。

房地产市场活跃度急剧下滑

高房贷利率正在重塑美国房地产市场格局。全美房地产经纪人协会数据显示，现房销售量已连续多月下跌，市场活跃度降至2010年以来最低水平。许多潜在买家被迫推迟购房计划，而卖家也面临定价压力。

Mortgage Rates Soar to Historic Highs

US mortgage rates are experiencing the most dramatic increase in nearly 20 years. According to Freddie Mac data, 30-year fixed-rate mortgages have surpassed the 7% threshold. Compared to the historical low of 2.65% in 2021, the increase exceeds 160%. This sharp change stems from the Federal Reserve's continued interest rate hikes and persistent inflationary pressures.

For ordinary homebuyers, this means a significant increase in homebuying costs. Taking a $500,000 house as an example with a 20% down payment, the current 7% interest rate would result in a monthly payment of approximately $2,661. Under the 2021 rate of 2.65%, the same conditions would require only $1,616 in monthly payments—a difference exceeding $1,000.

Real Estate Market Activity Plummets Sharply

High mortgage rates are reshaping the landscape of the US real estate market. According to data from the National Association of Realtors, existing home sales have declined for consecutive months, with market activity reaching its lowest level since 2010. Many potential buyers have been forced to postpone their homebuying plans, while sellers also face pricing pressure.
