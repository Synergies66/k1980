US Mortgage Rates Soar, Boston Housing Market Continues Downturn

K1980 深度 · 2026年04月10日
美国房贷利率再次大幅上升，大波士顿地区房地产市场持续低迷，预计将延续至2024年春季。高利率环境下，购房者观望情绪浓厚，房屋销量下滑，但房价仍保持相对坚挺。这一趋势不仅影响当地居民购房计划，也对在美工作的华人群体、投资者以及计划移居美国的人群产生重要影响。
US mortgage rates have surged again significantly, and the Greater Boston real estate market continues to experience a downturn, expected to persist through spring 2024. In the high-interest-rate environment, homebuyers adopt a wait-and-see attitude, with declining home sales, yet home prices remain relatively stable. This trend not only affects local residents' home purchase plans but also has significant implications for Chinese professionals working in the US, investors, and those planning to relocate to America.

房贷利率攀升重创购房需求

美国房贷利率在经历短暂回落后再次飙升，30年期固定房贷利率已突破7%关口，创20多年来新高。对于大波士顿地区这样的高房价市场，利率上升的冲击更为显著。一套价值80万美元的房屋，在当前利率水平下，月供比一年前增加约600-800美元，这直接抑制了潜在购房者的入市意愿。

对于在波士顿工作的华人群体而言，这意味着购房门槛进一步提高。许多原本计划在2024年购房的家庭可能需要重新评估财务状况，或考虑推迟购房计划。特别是那些持H1B签证的技术工作者，面临签证不确定性和高房贷成本的双重压力。

房地产市场进入观望期

数据显示，大波士顿地区房屋销售量同比下降超过20%，市场明显降温。但与销量形成对比的是，房价依然保持相对稳定，这主要得益于供应量的持续短缺。许多房主选择暂缓出售，因为他们享有的低利率房贷（通常在3-4%）远低于当前市场水平。

Rising Mortgage Rates Deal a Heavy Blow to Housing Demand

US mortgage rates have surged again after a brief decline, with 30-year fixed mortgage rates breaking through the 7% mark, reaching a new high in over 20 years. For high-price markets like Greater Boston, the impact of rising rates is particularly pronounced. For a home valued at $800,000, under current interest rate levels, monthly mortgage payments have increased by approximately $600-800 compared to a year ago, which directly suppresses potential homebuyers' market entry intentions.

For Chinese professionals working in Boston, this means the barrier to home purchase has risen further. Many families who originally planned to buy homes in 2024 may need to reassess their financial situation or consider postponing their purchase plans. Particularly for those holding H1B visas in tech positions, they face dual pressure from visa uncertainty and high mortgage costs.

Real Estate Market Enters Wait-and-See Period

Data shows that home sales in Greater Boston have declined more than 20% year-over-year, with a clear market slowdown. However, contrasting with the decline in sales volume is the fact that home prices remain relatively stable, primarily due to continued supply shortages. Many homeowners choose to delay selling because they enjoy low mortgage rates (typically 3-4%) that are far below current market levels.
