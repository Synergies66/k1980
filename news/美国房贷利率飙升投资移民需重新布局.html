美国房贷利率飙升，投资移民需重新布局

US Mortgage Rates Surge: Investment Immigration Requires Portfolio Restructuring

K1980 深度 · 2026年04月11日
随着美联储持续加息，美国房贷利率重回7%高位，波士顿等热门城市房价开始松动。对中国投资者而言，这既是挑战也是机遇：现有房产面临估值压力，但也为长期投资者提供了更好的入场时机。EB-5投资移民和留学家庭需要重新评估房产投资策略。
As the Federal Reserve continues raising interest rates, US 30-year mortgage rates have climbed back to 7%, causing prices in hot markets like Boston to soften. For Chinese investors, this presents both challenges and opportunities: existing properties face valuation pressure, but long-term investors gain better entry points. EB-5 investment immigrants and study-abroad families need to reassess their real estate investment strategies.

房贷利率创新高，市场降温明显

美国30年期固定房贷利率已攀升至7.1%，创2023年以来新高。相比2021年3%的历史低点，购房成本几乎翻倍。以50万美元房产为例，月供从之前的$2,108增至$3,360，年增加$15,000支出。

波士顿作为教育和科技中心，房价一直坚挺。但数据显示，2024年1月新房销售量同比下降18%，库存增加35%。剑桥、牛顿等华人聚集区域，挂牌时间延长至平均67天。

对中国投资者的直接冲击

持有美国房产的中国投资者面临三重压力：首先是资产估值下滑，特别是2020-2022年高位入场的投资者，账面损失可达10-15%；其次是持有成本上升，重新贷款面临更高利率；最后是出售困难，市场流动性明显下降。

对于计划投资的买家，现金流压力显著增加。原本预算100万美元的房产投资，现在实际购买力降至75万美元左右。

EB-5投资移民策略调整

EB-5投资者传统上偏爱房地产项目，但当前环境下需要更谨慎。开发商融资成本上升，项目周期延长，部分项目面临资金链紧张。建议重点关注：基础设施项目（政府背景更稳定）、已获得预售许可的成熟项目、位于人口增长区域的开发项目。

同时，直投EB-5项目（新农村试点计划）成为新选择，投资金额仅需80万美元，且有更强的政策保护。

留学家庭的购房时机

对于子女在美留学的家庭，当前可能是十年来最好的购房窗口。房价松动、议价空间增大，且卖家更愿意接受合理条件。波士顿周边学区房如Lexington、Brookline等地，价格回调5-10%。

但需注意现金流规划：如果需要贷款，建议锁定利率或选择ARM（可调利率贷款）前期较低的产品。

投资策略重新布局

短期内（6-18个月），建议采取防守策略：持有优质资产、避免高杠杆、关注现金流。对于新投资，重点考虑租金回报率高的区域，如近地铁的公寓楼盘。

中长期（2-5年），随着经济周期调整，房地产市场将迎来新机遇。历史经验显示，每次利率周期后的房价反弹都会创新高。提前布局人口流入区域、科技产业聚集地将获得超额收益。

政策风险与机遇并存

2024年是美国大选年，房地产相关政策可能调整。拜登政府的首次购房者补贴、外国买家税等提案都会影响市场。中国投资者需要密切关注政策动向，适时调整投资结构。

同时，美联储降息预期为下半年市场回暖提供支撑。一旦利率见顶回落，被抑制的购房需求将快速释放。

Mortgage Rates Hit New Highs, Market Clearly Cools

US 30-year fixed mortgage rates have climbed to 7.1%, reaching the highest level since 2023. Compared to the historic low of 3% in 2021, home purchase costs have nearly doubled. Taking a $500,000 property as an example, monthly mortgage payments have increased from $2,108 to $3,360, adding $15,000 in annual expenses.

Boston, as an education and technology hub, has maintained strong prices. However, data shows that January 2024 new home sales declined 18% year-over-year, with inventory increasing 35%. In Chinese-concentrated areas like Cambridge and Newton, average listing times have extended to 67 days.

Direct Impact on Chinese Investors

Chinese investors holding US properties face three-fold pressure: first, asset valuation declines, particularly for those who entered at peak prices between 2020-2022, facing paper losses of 10-15%; second, holding costs rise as refinancing faces higher rates; third, selling difficulties emerge as market liquidity significantly decreases.

For prospective buyers, cash flow pressure increases substantially. Properties budgeted at $1 million now offer purchasing power equivalent to only around $750,000.

EB-5 Investment Immigration Strategy Adjustments

EB-5 investors have traditionally favored real estate projects, but current conditions demand greater caution. Developer financing costs rise, project timelines extend, and some projects face funding chain pressures. Recommended focus areas include: infrastructure projects (government-backed stability), mature projects with pre-sale permits obtained, and development projects in population-growth regions.

Meanwhile, direct EB-5 investments (new rural pilot programs) emerge as new alternatives, requiring only $800,000 investment with stronger policy protections.

Purchase Timing for Study-Abroad Families

For families with children studying in America, now may be the best purchase window in a decade. Softening prices, increased negotiating room, and sellers more willing to accept reasonable terms create favorable conditions. School district homes near Boston like Lexington and Brookline have seen 5-10% price corrections.

However, note cash flow planning: if financing is needed, consider locking in rates or selecting ARM (adjustable-rate mortgage) products with lower initial rates.

Investment Strategy Restructuring

For the short term (6-18 months), adopt a defensive strategy: hold quality assets, avoid high leverage, focus on cash flow. For new investments, prioritize areas with high rental returns, such as apartment buildings near transit hubs.

Medium to long-term (2-5 years), as economic cycles adjust, real estate markets will encounter new opportunities. Historical experience shows each rate cycle's subsequent price recovery reaches new highs. Early positioning in population-inflow regions and technology industry clusters will generate excess returns.

Policy Risk and Opportunity Coexist

2024 is a US election year; real estate policies may adjust. The Biden administration's first-time homebuyer subsidies and foreign buyer taxes proposals will impact markets. Chinese investors must closely monitor policy developments and adjust investment structures timely.

Meanwhile, Federal Reserve rate-cut expectations support market recovery in the second half. Once rates peak and decline, suppressed home purchase demand will rapidly release.
